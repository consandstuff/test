SOUND VOLTEX#

Sound Voltex Booth was tested on various cities in Japan starting on August 26, 2011 until September 19, 2011. It was then released on January 18, 2012.

Overview#

Contrary to other rhythm games, in Sound voltex the players are not required to play the music correctly. Instead, players are challenged to make various effects on the existing music track.

The controller has 7 buttons (6 for gameplay and 1 start button) and 2 knobs to move two colored sliders on the screen.
